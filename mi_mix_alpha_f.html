MI MIX ALPHA is a Concept Mobile phone and It's Worlds first 5G Mobile and With wrap around display(7.9 inch).

It comes with Qualcomm snapdragon 855+ Processor.

It has Worlds first 108MP Samsung sensor.

Xiaomi Mi Mix Alpha smartphone has a OLED display. It measures 154.3 mm x 72.3 mm x 10.4 mm and weighs 241 grams. The screen has a resolution of 2088 x 2250 pixels and 389 ppi pixel density. On camera front, the buyers get a 108 MP + 20 MP + 12 MP Triple Primary Cameras and on the rear, there's an 108MP + 20MP + 12MP camera with features like Digital Zoom, Auto Flash, Face detection, Touch to focus. It is backed by a 4050 mAh battery. Connectivity features in the smartphone include WiFi, Bluetooth, GPS, Volte, and more.
